Shoping Guide

  • How to build a capsule wardrobe on a budget?


    A capsule wardrobe is a small collection of versatile pieces that can be mixed and matched to create a variety of looks. Building a capsule wardrobe on a budget can be a challenge, but it's definitely doable. First, let's talk about the basics. Every capsule wardrobe should include a few essential pieces, such as:
    A pair of jeans or trousers,
    A neutral-colored blouse or shirt,
    A neutral-colored cardigan or sweater,
    A versatile dress,
    A pair of neutral-colored shoes,
    These pieces can be mixed and matched to create a variety of looks. With these basics, you can start to add in some trendier pieces. For example, a few statement accessories, like a pair of earrings or a scarf, can really elevate a look. Or, you could add a pop of color with a bright-colored top or bottom. And don't forget about shoes! A pair of stylish shoes can really elevate a look. Just remember to keep it versatile and within your budget.

  • How to find sustainable fashion brands?

    When it comes to finding sustainable fashion brands, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, look for brands that use eco-friendly materials, like organic cotton, linen, or recycled polyester. Second, consider the brand's manufacturing practices. Do they have a transparent supply chain? Do they have fair labor practices? And finally, take a look at the brand's overall sustainability initiatives. Do they have a clear sustainability policy? Do they offset their carbon emissions? Keep these things in mind and you'll be well on your way to finding sustainable fashion brands

Style Tips

Here are a few of our favorite style tips:

  • Know your body type and dress accordingly.
  • Invest in classic pieces that will never go out of style.
  • Mix high and low pieces to create unique looks.
  • Play with proportions to create a flattering silhouette.
  • Accessorize to add personality and style to your look.
